Group Runs
Come join us at our weekly group runs! are organized year-round and anyone who is interested mayparticipate. You don't have to be a member of the HYP Running Club to participate. The run generally meet on Saturdays at 10:00AM on city Island and average 3-5 miles in legth. Begining and experienced of group runs are subject to change from week to week; email Fitness@hyp.org to be put on a list to receive the lastest on group runs.

Running Club Membership

HYP Sports and Fitness is pround to re-introduce the HYP Running Club! The Hyp Running Club is a friendly, supportive community within Harrisburg Young Professionals for anyone interested in social running. Novice runners training for their first 5K and experienced marathoners alike are welcome and encoraged to join us on our casual group runs.


HYP Running Club Highlights, New for 2017

  • FREE benefit of HYP membership
  • No membership restrictions (join whenever you want!)
  • Partnerships with local fitness experts and retailers (discounts!)
  • Discounts on all YMCA Race Series events
  • Easy registration throughRedSignUp.com (no more code!)


To join, simply visit http://runsignup.com/hyprun and click "SIGN UP". A(free) RedSignUp.com account is required to join and take advantage of discounted race registration. You can sign up with an existing RedSignUp.com account.

Prices on YMCA Series races increase after April 30, so if you are considering participating in any of these great events, join the HYP Running Club TODAY and clainyour discount!

The spirit of camaraderie remains the driving motivation behind the HYP Running Club. Our goal is to promote a welcoming, supportive, and inclusive environment as we share our enthusiasm for running with the entire Harrisburg Young Professionals community.
